## 🎯 30-Second Overview

**Before:** Simple flashcards with fixed review times
**Now:** AI-powered adaptive learning that knows when you'll forget

### Key Changes

1. **Hidden Memory Strength** - The app tracks how well you really know each word (0-100)
2. **Dynamic Intervals** - Reviews scheduled based on YOUR unique forgetting curve
3. **5 Study Modes** - From easy recognition to hard typing challenges
4. **Smart Task Selection** - The app picks the right difficulty for you
5. **Rich Analytics** - See your progress, streaks, and mastery levels

---

## 📱 How to Use

### Starting a Study Session

1. Open the **Study** tab (brain icon)
2. See how many words are "due now"
3. Click **"Begin Smart Review"**
4. Complete the tasks the app gives you

### During Study

You'll see different task types:

**Recognition** 🟢 (Easiest)
- See the word
- Pick the right meaning from 4 choices
- Good for new words

**Recall** 🔵 (Medium)
- See the word
- Try to remember the meaning
- Tap "Show Answer"
- Grade yourself: Forgot / Hard / Easy

**Listening** 🟡 (Harder)
- Listen to the word
- Type what you hear
- Tests pronunciation + spelling

**Typing** 🟠 (Hard)
- See the Chinese translation
- Type the English word
- Full active recall

**Sentence** 🔴 (Hardest)
- Create a sentence using the word
- Self-grade your understanding

### After Each Answer

The app:
1. ✅ Updates your **hidden memory strength**
2. 📈 Adjusts the next review time (could be 1 day or 180 days!)
3. 🎯 Picks the next task type based on how well you know it

## 💡 Pro Tips

### 1. **Trust the System**
The app schedules reviews based on science, not arbitrary times. If a word isn't showing up, you don't need to review it yet!

### 2. **Be Honest**
When self-grading (Recall/Sentence mode):
- **Forgot** = You couldn't recall it at all
- **Hard** = You eventually remembered, but struggled
- **Easy** = You knew it immediately

The algorithm adapts to your honesty.

### 3. **Embrace Harder Tasks**
- Typing and Listening give 2× the memory boost of Recognition
- The app will challenge you more as you improve
- It's okay to skip if you're not ready

### 4. **Study Daily (Even 5 Minutes)**
- Consistency > Long Sessions
- Daily reviews maintain your streak
- The algorithm works best with regular data

### 5. **Check Analytics**
Tap into analytics to see:
- Which words you're struggling with
- Your study streak
- Performance trends

## ❓ Common Questions

**Q: Why am I seeing a word I just studied?**
> If you clicked "Forgot" (quality 0), it resets the interval to review again soon. This is intentional for rapid re-learning.

**Q: Why do some words not appear for weeks?**
> High memory strength + stability = the algorithm knows you won't forget. Words with 85%+ strength can have 30-60+ day intervals.

**Q: Can I see my memory strength?**
> No - it's intentionally hidden to prevent "gaming" the system. Focus on honest self-assessment, and let the algorithm do its job.

**Q: What if I want to practice words that aren't due?**
> When all due words are done, you can click **"Practice Mode"** to review any words.

**Q: Do I need to change any settings?**
> Nope! Everything works automatically. Just study as usual.

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

**App feels too hard**
- The system adapts! Keep studying honestly
- You can skip difficult tasks if needed
- Accuracy matters more than speed

**App feels too easy**
- You're mastering the content! 🎉
- Intervals will lengthen automatically
- Add more challenging vocabulary

**Forgot a lot of words**
- That's okay! The system will reschedule them
- More frequent reviews will rebuild memory
- This is normal when returning after a break
